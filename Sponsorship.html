Why should you sponsor us?

  • Sponsoring F1 in Schools offers unparalleled global exposure, as the program is internationally recognized and attracts attention from students, educators, and motorsport enthusiasts worldwide.

  • By sponsoring F1 in Schools, companies invest in the development of future talent in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. Supporting such initiatives fosters a pipeline of skilled professionals for various industries, including engineering, automotive, and technology.

  • Associating a brand with F1 in Schools aligns it with innovation, excellence, and cutting-edge technology. This association can enhance brand reputation and perception among target audiences, showcasing the company's commitment to education and advancement.

  • Sponsoring F1 in Schools demonstrates a commitment to community engagement and education. It provides opportunities for employees to volunteer, mentor students, and engage with local schools, fostering a positive relationship with the community and enhancing corporate social responsibility efforts.

  • Sponsors of F1 in Schools gain access to various marketing opportunities, including branding on competition materials, digital platforms, and event promotions. This exposure allows companies to reach a diverse audience of students, parents, educators, and industry professionals.

  • F1 in Schools challenges students to design, build, and race miniature F1 cars, promoting innovation and creativity. Sponsors can showcase their own innovative products, technologies, and solutions through collaborations, demonstrations, and exhibitions at events, highlighting their role in driving progress and advancement.

  • Sponsoring F1 in Schools provides companies with access to a pool of talented and ambitious students who may become future employees. Engaging with participants through mentorship, internships, and networking opportunities can facilitate recruitment efforts and help identify promising candidates for future roles within the organisation.

  • Supporting F1 in Schools can have a lasting impact on the lives and careers of participating students. By investing in their education and personal development, sponsors contribute to the growth of future leaders and innovators, leaving a positive legacy in the community and the wider industry.

Sponsorship Benefits

We have 5 tiers of sponsorship which include their respective benefits based on the type and value of the sponsorship.

Kind Sponsorship

In this type of sponsorship no money is paid by you but you provides us access to the resources and guidance required for the completion.

•We will post up to 2 advertisements about your brand of your choice on our social media platforms.

•Mention of our brand on our website’s sponsors section.

•Mention of thanks in the verbal presentation.

•Your logo will be displayed on our car, uniform and pit display posters.

Note:

Additional benefits may apply based on the value of the service provided.*

Benefits included:

Bronze Level Sponsorship

In this level of sponsorship you pay us a sum of money ranging from ₹1,000/- to ₹10,000/-.

•We will post up to 5 advertisements about your brand of your choice on our social media platforms.

•Mention of our brand on our website’s sponsors section.

•Mention of thanks in the verbal presentation.

•Your logo will be displayed on our car, uniform and pit display posters with a higher priority than kind sponsorships.

Benefits included:

Silver Level Sponsorship

In this level of sponsorship you pay us a sum of money ranging from ₹10,000/- to ₹25,000/-.

•We will post up to 10 advertisements about your brand of your choice on our social media platforms.

•Mention of our brand on our website’s sponsors section.

•Mention of thanks in the verbal presentation.

•Your logo will be displayed on our car, uniform and pit display posters with a higher priority than kind and bronze level sponsorships.

Benefits included:

Gold Level Sponsorship

In this level of sponsorship you pay us a sum of money ranging from ₹25,000/- to ₹50,000/-.

•We will post up to 15 advertisements about your brand of your choice on our social media platforms.

•Mention of our brand on our website’s sponsors section.

•Mention of thanks in the verbal presentation.

•Your logo will be displayed on our car, uniform and pit display posters with a higher priority than kind, bronze and Silver level sponsorships. Your logo will be displayed on both sides of our uniform (front and back).

•Your logo will be displayed on the first and last slides of all our presentations.

Benefits included:

Diamond Level Sponsorship

In this level of sponsorship you pay us a sum of money of ₹50,000/- or above.

•We will post up to 30 advertisements about your brand of your choice on our social media platforms.

•Mention of our brand on our website’s sponsors section.

•Mention of thanks and one short detailed paragraph about you in our verbal presentation.

•Your logo will be displayed on our car, uniform and pit display posters with a higher priority than kind, bronze, silver, gold level sponsorships. You will have the largest logo on our car and uniform and your logo will be displayed on all both sides of our car (left and right) and uniform (front and back).

•Your logo will be displayed on the first and last slides of all our presentations.
